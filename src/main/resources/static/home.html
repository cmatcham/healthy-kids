eat·move·sleep

Helen and Amos transformed their own lives when they changed how they cared for their bodies. Now they do that for their kids, making sure that healthy eating, movement, and sleep are just part of their family’s routine.

Meal times in the Amosa household are special family times. Helen and Amos have put a lot of thought into how to make sure everyone eats a good variety of healthy food that they enjoy.
Helen and Amos love getting out with their kids, be it a game in the park or a bike ride to the shops. But they’ve also found lots of fun ways for their kids to be active indoors.
Helen and Amos believe that the way children go to sleep is the way they wake up. They’ve set up routines to make sure their children go to bed feeling safe, comfortable, and relaxed.
