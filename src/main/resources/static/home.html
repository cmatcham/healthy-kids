Are you interested in making changes that will help your family feel better and have more fun together? Healthy Kids is for you. It’s free, easy and anyone can play. Would you like to learn more?

The Healthy Kids game lets you set goals for how you and your family eat, move, and sleep. You can decide on how you will reward yourselves for reaching your goals, and you can track everyone’s progress over time. If you want, you can share your family’s success with other people.

Only you will be able to track and share your family’s progress. Logging in is only so you can record your family’s data.

Would you and your family like to play the Healthy Kids game? Then go back to the Welcome page to join or sign in.
